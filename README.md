# Capital One Mobile · VoC Health Monitor

Weekly monitoring dashboard tracking app health based on Google Play reviews.

## Overview
- **Data source:** Google Play Store (public), US locale
- **App:** Capital One Mobile (`com.konylabs.capitalone`)
- **Period:** May 2025 – April 2026
- **Volume:** 29,000+ reviews

## Business Question
What is the current health of the Capital One Mobile app, and where are users experiencing problems?

## Dashboard
[View on Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/julia6345/viz/DASH_Capital_One/CapitalOneAndroidAppVoCMonitor)

## Tools
- Python (google-play-scraper, pandas)
- Tableau Public

## Key Findings
- App Stability is the #1 complaint driver across all versions
- Versions 6.30.4 and 6.31.10 (Sep–Oct 2025) had the worst ratings (3.76–3.79)
- Rating recovered to 4.28 with v6.41.6 (Apr 2026)
